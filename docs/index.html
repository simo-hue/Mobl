Capabilities

Designed for Your Purchase Archives

Scan Receipts & Invoices

Use the native iOS document scanner to capture receipts before ink fades, or import PDFs directly from your Files application.

Track Warranty Deadlines

Input warranty duration and purchase dates. The app calculates exact expiration dates and highlights critical timelines.

Smart Local Reminders

Receive automatic notifications on your device before a return window closes or a manufacturer warranty expires.

Face ID / Touch ID Security

Secure your purchase data. Enable optional biometric authentication to keep invoices and receipts protected from prying eyes.

Export individual receipts as high-quality PDFs or output a compressed ZIP backup containing your entire structural data archive.

No cloud services required. All processing, document scanning, and text recognition occurs strictly on your device.
