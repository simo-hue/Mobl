# Warranty Vault Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Warranty Vault is designed as a local-first app. The app stores receipts, invoices, warranty documents, product photos, prices, notes, and reminders locally on your device.

The developer does not receive your documents, prices, notes, purchase data, or personal information. The app does not require an account, does not include advertising SDKs, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan a document. Photo library and Files access are used only when you choose to import documents or images. Face ID or Touch ID is optional and is used only to protect access to the app on your device. Notifications are local reminders scheduled on your device.

Exports are created only when you choose to export or share data. Device backups depend on your iOS and iCloud backup settings and on the in-app backup preference.

You can delete individual purchases and documents in the app, delete all app data from Settings, or delete the app from your device.

For App Store Connect privacy details, the intended v1 declaration is:

- Tracking: No
- Data collected by the developer: None
- Third-party data sharing: None

If future versions add external services, analytics, cloud sync, support tooling, or crash reporting, this policy and the App Store privacy details must be updated before release.
